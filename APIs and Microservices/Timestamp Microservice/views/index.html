Timestamp Microservice

This microservive will convert the given time into Unix and UTC timestamp!

In order to convert the given time, we have to visit the URL in following format:

[project url]/api/timestamp/[date string]

Example Usage:

[project url]/api/timestamp/2019-12-25 [project url]/api/timestamp/1579356829

Example Output:

{"unix":1579356829,"utc":"Mon, 19 Jan 1970 06:42:36 GMT"}
